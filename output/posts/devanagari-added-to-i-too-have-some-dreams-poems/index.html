Devanagari added to I Too Have Some Dreams poems

A. Sean Pue

2015-08-18 14:42

Source

I have added Devanagari (Hindi) versions of N. M. Rashed’s Urdu poems to the Resource Webpage for I Too Have Some Dreams.

These are being generated from the same transliteration that makes the Urdu and romanized versions. There will be a few errors, but they are looking pretty good!
